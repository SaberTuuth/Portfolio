About Me

I'm Kasey Russell, a passionate game developer focused on creating immersive, systems-driven gameplay using C++ with Unreal Engine and C# with Unity. I specialize in interactive mechanics, smart AI behaviors, and responsive systems that make each session feel dynamic and engaging. I take pride in writing clean, modular code and thrive in both collaborative and solo development environments. My mission is to design unique experiences that not only play well but feel alive—games where every mechanic supports player freedom, creativity, and challenge.

My development philosophy centers on gameplay-first design, where every feature is built to enhance the player's agency and immersion. I strive to create systems that feel intuitive, polished, and alive—whether I’m designing challenging enemy behaviors, interactive mechanics, or dynamic, reactive environments. I take pride in writing clean, modular code that’s easy to scale and maintain, allowing for seamless iteration and long-term project health. I believe that great gameplay emerges through tight feedback loops, where constant testing and player input shape and refine the experience. Collaboration is at the heart of my process—I value open communication with artists, designers, and other developers to bring shared visions to life. My goal is always to create gameplay systems that not only work technically but feel purposeful and exciting, encouraging players to explore, experiment, and stay engaged.

I earned my degree from Full Sail University, where I developed strong skills in C++ and C#, along with a versatile toolkit essential for both game development and software engineering. My education emphasized hands-on, project-based learning, giving me experience in problem-solving, rapid prototyping, version control, Java, SQL, UI/UX design, and more. I’m constantly learning and adapting—exploring new techniques, tools, and frameworks to sharpen my abilities and stay current in a fast-moving industry. Whether it's diving into a new engine feature or refining a custom gameplay system, I'm driven by a desire to keep improving and creating more engaging experiences. Outside of active development, I enjoy analyzing games, deconstructing systems, and experimenting with mechanics to understand what makes gameplay feel satisfying and impactful. I believe that great games are built with both creativity and discipline—and I bring that mindset into everything I create.
